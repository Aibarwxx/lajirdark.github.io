Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions our students ask most often

How do I enrol in a course?

Create a free account on our Register page, then browse the course catalogue. Click Enrol on any course page and you will have immediate access to all materials — no waiting period required.

What is the refund policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If a course does not meet your expectations within the first thirty days, contact our support team and we will process a full refund without question.

How long do I have access to course materials?

Enrolment grants you lifetime access to all lectures, readings, and supplementary resources. There are no expiry dates, so you can study at your own pace and return to any material whenever it suits you.

Are certificates awarded on completion?

Yes. Students who successfully complete all graded work receive a digitally signed certificate of completion from Academia Classica. These credentials are recognised by academic institutions and professional bodies in over 40 countries.

Can I interact with instructors directly?

Every course includes a moderated discussion forum. Instructors participate actively and aim to respond to student questions within 48 hours on business days. Live Q&A webinars are also held monthly for enrolled students.

What are the technical requirements?

You need a stable internet connection and a modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge are all fully supported. Our platform is responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and smartphone alike.

How and when are assignments graded?

Qualified instructors review and return graded assignments with written feedback within 7 to 10 business days of submission. Certain courses also incorporate a structured peer-review component to broaden perspectives.

Can credits be transferred to a university?

Credit transfer policies differ by institution. We provide detailed syllabi, learning outcomes, and instructor credentials to support any application you make. We recommend consulting your academic registrar before enrolling if credit transfer is a priority.

Is financial assistance available?

Yes. We offer means-tested scholarships and flexible instalment payment plans. Visit the Financial Aid section after logging in, or email our admissions office at admissions@academiaclassica.edu to discuss your options.

How do I reset a forgotten password?

Click Forgot Password on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions in the reset email. If the email does not arrive within five minutes, please check your spam folder or contact support.
